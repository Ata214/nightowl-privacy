🦉 Night Owl — Privacy Policy

Last updated: March 4, 2026

Overview

Night Owl ("we", "the app") is a blue light filter and screen dimmer application. We respect your privacy and are committed to protecting your personal data.

Data Collection

Night Owl does not collect, store, or transmit any personal data. All settings (filter preferences, language, schedules, profiles) are stored locally on your device only using SharedPreferences.

Third-Party Services

The app uses the following third-party services:

  • Google AdMob — Displays ads in the free version. AdMob may collect device identifiers, IP address, and usage data. See Google's Privacy Policy.
  • Google Play Billing — Processes premium purchases. Purchase data is handled by Google. See Google Payments Privacy Notice.

Permissions

The app requests the following permissions:

  • Display over other apps (SYSTEM_ALERT_WINDOW) — Required to show the screen filter overlay.
  • Foreground Service — Required to keep the filter running and manage the schedule.
  • Internet — Required for displaying ads and processing purchases.
  • Notifications — Required to show the persistent notification while the filter is active.

In-App Purchases

Night Owl offers a premium upgrade to remove ads. Purchase transactions are processed entirely by Google Play. We do not collect or store any payment information.

Children's Privacy

The app is not directed at children under 13. We do not knowingly collect data from children.

Changes

We may update this policy from time to time. Changes will be reflected on this page with an updated date.
